# RaidScout DKP Tutorial — Full Script

> **Duration:** ~8 minutes  
> **Tone:** Friendly, clear, instructional  
> **Setup:** Have RaidScout open with a test server that has DKP enabled, a few boss kills recorded, and one item ready to mark for bid.

---

## INTRO (0:00–0:30)

"Welcome to RaidScout. In this video I'm going to walk you through our DKP system — how to claim your character, how DKP points work, and how the bidding system lets your guild distribute loot fairly."

"There are three things we'll cover: claiming your character, understanding DKP points, and the bidding system. Let's jump in."

---

## PART 1: CLAIM YOUR CHARACTER (0:30–1:45)

### Step 1 — Go to Join a Server

"Before you can earn or spend DKP, you need to link your RaidScout account to your in-game character. Head over to the Join a Server page."

[Show clicking "Join a Server" or navigating to /join]

### Step 2 — Search for your character

"Type your in-game character name in the search box. RaidScout will look for matching names across the server's member list."

[Type a name, show results appearing]

### Step 3 — Submit your claim

"Once you find your name, click the Claim button. This sends a request to your server's staff — the owner or a moderator — asking them to verify that this is really you."

[Click Claim, show the pending state]

### Step 4 — Staff approves the claim

"On the staff side, they'll see a notification badge in the top bar showing how many pending claims there are. They can click it, review your request, and accept or decline it."

[Switch to staff account briefly, show Claims badge, accept the claim]

### Step 5 — You're linked

"Once accepted, you're linked. You'll see a green checkmark, and now you can participate in DKP auctions and earn points from boss kills."

[Show the green checkmark on the user account]

---

## PART 2: HOW DKP POINTS WORK (1:45–3:30)

### Where DKP comes from

"So where do DKP points actually come from? There are two ways: earning and adjustments."

**Earning from boss kills:**
"When your guild kills a boss, RaidScout can automatically award DKP points to everyone who participated. The amount depends on your server's DKP multiplier setting — your staff configures this in Server Settings."

[Show the DKP Settings page briefly — point out the multiplier]

**Staff adjustments:**
"Staff can also manually adjust a member's DKP balance — adding or deducting points. This is done through the leaderboard using the plus-minus button next to any member."

[Show the ± button on the leaderboard, demonstrate adding points]

### Where DKP goes

"DKP is spent through the bidding system, which we'll cover next. When you place a bid and win, your DKP is deducted. If you lose or get outbid, your DKP is automatically refunded."

### Viewing your balance

"Your DKP balance is always visible at the top of the DKP page. You can see your available balance, total earned all-time, and total spent all-time. Clicking any member in the leaderboard shows their full transaction history."

[Show the DKP Ledger card and the leaderboard]

---

## PART 3: THE BIDDING SYSTEM (3:30–7:00)

### 3A — Staff marks an item for bid

"Bidding starts when staff marks an item for auction. Let me show you how that works."

[As staff, click "Mark Item for Bid"]

"There are a few things you configure when marking an item:"

- "The item name — just search for it from the catalog"
- "The starting DKP cost — this is the minimum bid"
- "The duration — how long the auction runs. The default comes from your DKP settings"
- "Quantity — if you have multiple copies of an item"
- "Guild restriction — optional. If you pick a guild, only members of that guild can bid"

[Fill in the form, click Mark for Bid]

"The item now appears in the Live Auction panel with a countdown timer. Everyone in the server can see it."

### 3B — Members place bids

"Now let's switch to a regular member account and place a bid."

[Switch to member account]

"Each active auction shows the item name, its rarity color, the current highest bid, how many bids have been placed, and the time remaining. Click the Bid button to open the bidding modal."

[Click Bid]

"The modal shows your available balance, the minimum bid, and preset buttons to quickly add common amounts. You can also type a custom amount."

[Place a bid]

"Once you bid, you'll see a green 'You're Winning' badge if you're the highest bidder. Your DKP is held for this bid — it's deducted from your available balance."

### 3C — Getting outbid

"Now here's where it gets interesting. Let me switch to another account and place a higher bid."

[On second account, place a higher bid]

"Back on the first account — you'll immediately get a toast notification in the bottom-right corner. It tells you the current bid and that your DKP has been refunded. Your points are back in your balance, and you can bid again right away."

[Show the toast banner, point out the refund message]

"Clicking the toast takes you directly to the item so you can place a new bid."

### 3D — Auction ends

"When the timer runs out, the auction resolves automatically. The highest bidder wins the item and gets a notification. Everyone else gets their DKP refunded."

[Show the item moving to Auction History]

"Staff can also manually resolve or cancel an auction at any time using the Cancel button."

### 3E — Auction history

"All past auctions appear in the Auction History section below the live auctions. You can see who won, the winning bid amount, how many bids there were, and when the auction ran. Staff can mark items as distributed with the checkmark toggle."

---

## PART 4: DKP LEADERBOARD & TOOLS (7:00–7:45)

### Leaderboard

"The leaderboard ranks all members by their current DKP balance. You can search for specific members, filter by guild, and click any row to see their full transaction history."

[Show search, guild filter, clicking a member]

### Help tooltip

"If you're ever unsure how DKP works, click the question mark icon next to the search bar. It explains that points come from boss kills, adjustments by staff, and are spent through bids."

[Click the ? button]

### Reset DKP (staff only)

"Staff can reset DKP for a new season using the Reset button. A confirmation modal appears with a guild checklist — you pick which guilds to reset. Typing 'confirm' prevents accidents. All history is preserved — the reset just zeroes out current balances."

[Show the reset flow briefly]

---

## OUTRO (7:45–8:00)

"That's the complete DKP system in RaidScout. Claim your character, earn points from boss kills, bid on items fairly, and let the system handle refunds and notifications automatically."

"If you have questions, check the help tooltip on the DKP page or reach out to your server staff. Thanks for watching."
